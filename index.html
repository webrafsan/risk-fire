Resident Fire Safety Guide

Article 21A - Your Rights (Fire Safety Act 2021)

As a resident, you have the legal right to request and receive information about fire safety measures in your building. This includes:

  • Access to the most recent Fire Risk Assessment and related documentation
  • Information about the responsible person for fire safety management
  • Details of fire safety measures and any ongoing remedial works
  • Regular updates on compliance status and safety improvements
  • Notification of any significant changes to fire safety arrangements

Need more information? Contact your property manager using the details above or visit gov.uk/fire-safety-advice for comprehensive guidance on your rights and fire safety in residential buildings.

Emergency Procedures

In Case of Fire:

  • 1. Sound the alarm by activating the nearest manual call point
  • 2. Leave the building immediately via the nearest available exit
  • 3. Close all doors behind you as you evacuate
  • 4. Proceed directly to the designated assembly point
  • 5. Call 999 once you are safely outside the building
  • 6. Do not re-enter the building until authorized by the Fire Service

Assembly Point: Main car park area, north side near the main entrance gate
